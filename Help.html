Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for courses?

You can register for courses by logging into the student portal via the "Go to Login" button on our homepage. Once logged in, navigate to the course registration page, select your level, branch, and semester, and choose your courses according to the credit requirements.

Where can I find my course schedule?

Your finalized course schedule, including class times and locations, will be available in your student dashboard one week before the semester begins. An email notification will be sent to you once it is ready.

How do I apply for financial aid?

Information regarding financial aid, scholarships, and application procedures can be found on the "Admissions" section of our website. For direct assistance, please contact the Financial Aid office.

What if I forget my login password?

If you forget your password, you can use the "Forgot password?" link on the login form. This will guide you through the process of resetting your password via your registered email address.
